## Category 1: Girokonten (Checking Accounts)

**Card 1**
Front: What are the three main Girokonto tiers at BBBank?
Back: Easy, Komfort, Premium (plus Gemeinschaftskonto for couples)

**Card 2**
Front: Girokonto Easy typical monthly fee?
Back: ~€0-€3 (often free for students/under 25)

**Card 3**
Front: Girokonto Komfort typical monthly fee?
Back: ~€5-€8

**Card 4**
Front: Girokonto Premium includes what extra features?
Back: Concierge service, travel insurance, higher ATM withdrawal limits globally, sometimes free foreign transactions

**Card 5**
Front: Which Girokonto includes free additional cards?
Back: Gemeinschaftskonto (joint account) usually includes 1-2 free extra cards

## Category 2: Savings Products

**Card 6**
Front: Tagesgeld interest rate range (typical)?
Back: 1-2% variable (check current rate daily)

**Card 7**
Front: Festgeld 1-12 months rate vs. Tagesgeld?
Back: Usually +0.5-1% higher than Tagesgeld

**Card 8**
Front: Festgeld 2-5 years rate vs. shorter Festgeld?
Back: +0.2-0.5% higher (longer term = better rate)

**Card 9**
Front: Kinderkonto typical minimum deposit?
Back: €0-€50 opened by parents, child gets own card at 13+

**Card 10**
Front: Tax on savings interest (Abgeltungssteuer)?
Back: 25% + solidarity surcharge (+ church tax if applicable) — automatically withheld by bank

## Category 13: Typical Questions & Answers

**Card 58**
Front: "Which account is cheapest for me?"
Back: Depends on age, income, ATM usage, card needs. Use price comparison tool; recommend cheapest that fits requirements.

**Card 59**
Front: "How much can I borrow for a house?"
Back: Typically 80-90% of property value (Beleihung). Need 10-20% down. Exact amount = income × multiplier (usually 3-4x annual net).

**Card 60**
Front: "Is Bauspar worth it?"
Back: Good for disciplined savers who want guaranteed loan later; worse if want flexibility or higher returns (then ETF-Finanzierung).
