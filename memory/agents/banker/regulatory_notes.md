# Regulatory Knowledge — BBBank

## Key Laws & Regulations

### § 64 WpHG — Anlageberatung (Investment Advice)
- **Core:** Suitability requirement (Geeignetheitsprüfung)
- **Must assess:** Knowledge, experience, financial situation, investment objectives
- **Document:** Geeignetheitserklärung (suitability statement)
- **Liability:** Advice must be appropriate for client profile

### MiFID II (EU)
- Product governance obligations
- Cost transparency (ex-ante, ex-post)
- Best execution
- Inducement rules (no third-party payments influencing advice)

### KWG (Kreditwesengesetz)
- Banking license requirements
- Compliance obligations
- BaFin supervision

### ZAG (Zahlungsdiensteaufsichtsgesetz)
- Payment services regulation
- PSD2 implementation

## Compliance Must-Knows

**Before Every Product Recommendation:**
1. ✓ Is this suitable for the client's risk profile?
2. ✓ Do they understand the product?
3. ✓ Are costs and risks clearly explained?
4. ✓ Is documentation complete?

**Never Do:**
- Promise specific returns
- Recommend without suitability check
- Accept instructions from unauthorized persons
- Mix personal/client funds

**Always Do:**
- Document advice in writing
- Obtain written consent for transactions
- Report suspicious activities (MLA suspicions)
- Continue education (Weiterbildungspflicht)
