# DZ Bank Ecosystem — Knowledge Base

## DZ Bank AG (Central Institution)
- Umbrella for ~700 local cooperative banks (VR-Banken)
- Wholesale banking, capital markets, product factory
- Not customer-facing — you sell their products

## Product Partners

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Type** | Investment funds, asset management |
| **Key Products** | UniGlobal, UniRak, UniFavorit, Immobilienfonds |
| **Why It Matters** | Core product for Vermögensaufbau (wealth building) |
| **Quick Fact** | Germany's largest cooperative asset manager |

### R+V Versicherung
| Aspect | Details |

## Client Conversation Framework

**Wealth Building (Vermögensaufbau)**
1. R+V: Protect what you have (insurance)
2. Schwäbisch Hall: Save for home (Bauspar)
3. Union Investment: Grow surplus (funds)
4. DZ HYP: Leverage into property (mortgage)

**The Cooperative Advantage Story**
- "We don't have shareholders—we have members"
- "Profits stay in the region"
- "You own a piece of this bank"
